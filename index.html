Capstone Project with Squirrel AI

Overview

This capstone project is part of the METALS program at Carnegie Mellon University. Our team partnered with Squirrel AI to improve the learning and user experience of their online learning platform. Through a rigorous research process, including qualitative research with experts, teachers, and learners and quantitative research with educational data mining, weâ€™ve identified the needs and challenges of the users and thus some possible areas of improvement for the mistake analysis system on the Squirrel AI online learning platform. Our proposed solution will help middle school students to analyze, understand, and reflect on their mistakes; thereby developing their meta-cognitive skills.
